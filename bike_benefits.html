Bike Benefits Santa Fe

Data vis explainer based on 2018 Santa Fe Bike Master Plan

Created as part of a masters course work for MCAD’S Sustainability in Design program, this explainer infographic draws upon public data from Santa Fe’s Bicycle Master Plan to visually explain the 110 page document and the importance of supporting cycling infrastructure, and particularly how it relates to access. The goal was to educate residents about how their actions to ride a bike can make simple changes to help ease the economic and environmental impact of transport where the BMP may not be accessible or understandable to the broader residential population. Self driven, MA project. Designed and concepted.
