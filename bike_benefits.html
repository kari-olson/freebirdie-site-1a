Bike Benefits Santa Fe

Data vis project based on 2018 Santa Fe Bike Master Plan

Created as part of a masters course work for MCAD’S Sustainability in Design program, this infographic draws upon public data from Santa Fe’s Bicycle Master Plan to visually display the impact of cycling, and particularly how it relates to access. The goal was to educate residents about how their actions to ride a bike can make simple changes to help ease the economic and environmental impact of transport. Self driven, MA project. Designed and concepted.
