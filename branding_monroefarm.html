Monroe Farm

Delivering a refreshed presence to Monroe Farm in Kersey, CO

Monroe Farm is one of the oldest organic farms in Colorado, established in 1936. Faced with the challenge of connecting to an urban audience from a remote location, we refreshed an identity that would embrace their current philosophy as well as “organically” help spread the word through apparel and signage.
