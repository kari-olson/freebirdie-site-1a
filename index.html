ABOUT

My name is Kari Olson and I’m a graphic designer and illustrator. I am experienced in both print and web design with 10+ years of experience and am passionate about branding/identity, editorial and packaging design.

My Fine Arts degree is in Community Art and informs my graphic design work; I am skilled in traditional artistic media and also know how to strategically solve complex design problems. My goal is to produce user-centred work that resonates with the public and the client, and is also super creative and exciting. Because why be boring?
